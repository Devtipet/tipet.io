About Tipet

Tipet is a platform built on Blockchain with the major aim to revolutionize global Healthcare systems. We promote the standard operations of healthcare services ranging from data sharing, health records and access to timely and easy supply chain management of pharmaceuticals, drug, medical supply and drugs prescription. Tipet ensures greater transparency in payment frequency and occurrence, ease of cross-platform data sharing and lowering barriers to entry into the healthcare systems. Global health built on Blockchain.
